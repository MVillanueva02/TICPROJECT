Recipe #1: Lasagna

  • Time: 5h
  • Servings: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Lasagna Sheets (storebought or homemade) A package worth.
Ground pork and beef 1kg
Chopped tomatoes Around 800g
Onion One large
Garlic Three cloves
Celery One stock
Carrots Two or three, depending on size
Kosher Salt and freshly ground pepper To taste
Basil (add other herbs if you feel like it) A bunch (half a cup?)
Tomato Paste One tablespoon
Extra Virgin Olive Oil Couple of tablespoons
White wine About a cup
Whole Milk Four cups
Butter 3 tablespoons
AP Flour 3 tablespoons
Parmesan (freshly grated) The whole thing

Step 1:season the ground meat and shape it into meatballs, more or less the size of a golf ball.

Step 2:grease the bottom of a pot with oil and put it over high heat. Sear the meatballs.

Step 3:let the meatballs rest on a rack and sautee your sofrito (mirepoix) until the onions turn translucentand the veggies are cooked.Season and add the garlic. Sautee with tomato paste for a couple minutes. Deglaze with white wine.

Step 4:add tomatoes, milk and meatballs and let simmer 2-2.30h at low heat.

Step 5:heat up the butter in a saucepan and add the flour. After the flour is incorporated slowly start streaming in the milk until a b√©chamel forms. Season to taste. Add some parmesan cheese.

Step 6:par cook the pasta in salted water untill just under al dente.

Step 7:When the sauce is ready tear apart the meatballs and pepare a pan for assembly. Start with a light layer of sauce, and then add you pasta. On top, sauce and bechamel, repeat untill pan is full and finish with a layer of parmesan.

Step 8:bake in the oven untill ready, and wait at least twenty minutes before serving.

Recipe #2: Carbonara

  • Time: 45min
  • Servings: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Semolina Pasta (any elonged shape) A package worth.
Guanciale (bacon or pancetta will do too) 200g
Eggs 6 large
Kosher Salt and freshly ground pepper To taste
Parmesan (freshly grated) The whole thing

Step 1:in a pan, sautee guanciale untill it has rendered most of its fat. Take out of th heat

Step 2:in salted water, boil the pasta untill just shy of al dente.

Step 3:beat all of the eggs and add the grated parmesan. Season with PEPPER.

Step 4:Add the pasta to the pan, and when its cooled down, add the eggs. Mix the up and bring them to temperature slowly while moving constantly.

Step 5:plate and season with more parmesan on top.

Recipe #3: Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

  • Time: 25-30min
  • Servings: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti A package worth.
Extra virgin olive oil half a cup
Garlic four or five cloves
Kosher Salt To taste
Pepperoncino (pepper flakes) To taste

Step 1:heat up the oil in a pan, and add thinly sliced garlic. Sautee untill translucent, but do not let it pick up any color.

Step 2:before the garlic is done, boil pasta in salted water untill al dente.

Step 3:add pepperoncino to the pan, and add the pasta. Take off the heat.

Step 4:Plate.
